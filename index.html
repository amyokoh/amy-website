Describe legal guidelines and constraints that impact digital projects – How does GDPR impact digital projects?

As a developer, you must follow the GDPR compliance when dealing with customer datas. You must get permission from the customer before storing or using their datas and there should an opt-out button for your customer to click incase they want out. You should only hold datas that you need and delete the once not needed. You should have sufficient security to prevent datas been leak either accidentally or deliberately. Staff should be trained to avoid data breach.You have to be ready to answer to breach if there is any breach.
